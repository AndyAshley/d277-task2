Syracuse, New York

Syracuse is a city in Central New York. Once a fontier town, it is now bustling with a rich history and a diverse population. Syracuse is known for its universities, museums, and festivals.

City Facts

  • Population: 146,781
  • Incorporated in: 1820
  • Region: Finger Lakes Region
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: Below State Average @ $40,490
