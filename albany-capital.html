Albany, New York -State Capital

First settled by the Dutch in 1614, Albany is the oldest continuous settlement of the original 13 colonies and the longest continuously chartered city in the United States.

City Facts

  • Population: 99,402
  • Incorporated in: 1686
  • Region: Capital Region
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: Below State Average @ $52,583
