Alexandria Bay, New York

Alexandria Bay is a village in Jefferson County, New York, and is located to the north in the Thousand Islands region. It is home to the Thousand Islands Bridge, which connects New York State to the province of Ontario in Canada.

City Facts

  • Population: 1,097
  • Incorporated in: 1839
  • Region: Thousand Islands Region
  • Classification: Rural
  • Average Income: Below State Average @ $43,264

Things to do Alexandria Bay

Alexandria Bay is a popular tourist destination in the summer months. It is known for its boat tours of the Thousand Islands, Boldt Castle, and the Antique Boat Museum. Here are some places to see on your visit:

  1. Boldt Castle Tour
  2. Pirate Days Festival
  3. Thousand Islands Winery
  4. Scenic Boat Tours
  5. Dark Island Spirits Distillery
