New York State Overview -The Empire State

Residing in the northeastern United States, New York was one of the original thirteen colonies established in 1664. With over 20 million residents, it is the fourth most populous state. It's geography varies greatly, from the Adirondack Mountains in the north, to the Great Lakes to the west, to all the platues and lowlands in between. New York City, the largest city in the United States, is located in the southeastern part of the state. The state is known for its diverse culture, and is a major center for finance, fashion, and the arts.
